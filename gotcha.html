Gotcha! Watcha! is a book of warnings, in poetic form, some light-hearted, some heavy, all deadly serious.

Drugs are deadly serious.

Can I convince anybody of anything—especially with peer pressures and the desire to go along with the crowd? Maybe not, but if I could convince you of anything it would be to weigh the question of drugs, think about the dangers and implications.

 

 This book uses a light touch to deal with a heavy subject. if one young reader is made aware of the dangers of drug abuse by reading this book, it will have done its job.

-Dennis Held, poet

 

 Reading these poems reminds me why I am so proud to be sober. They speak the difficult truth in a biting, yet sometimes light-hearted way. The characters here come to life and several remind me of my goofy self, trapped by drugs and falling so fast that some would see it as humorous, if not for its terrible nature. Gotcha! Watcha! incorporates the harsh experiences of the real drug world—funny and tragic.

-Kyle F., recovering addict
